About Launch Tech

Launch Tech is a SaaS (Software as a Service) product holding company, created for launching real-world software applications. We create products that are useful, meaningful, and applicable to different people and different situations around the world. We try to build, market, and deploy software through partnerships, events, and sponsors.

Though we are a SaaS product company, we try to open with our processes, builds, and deadlines. We have a calendar that shares all of the things we're working on, and when certain events/product-builds will start, end, and be deployed.
