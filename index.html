About Launch Tech

Launch Tech is a project incubator that helps students design, build, market, and grow their projects/ventures. We provide assistance in the creation, growth, and maintenence of products, and we provide our clients resources that are extremely hard to find when you're just starting out.

We are dedicated to helping beginners build their ideas into a product, and accelerating experts to grow their products into a startup.

Our Projects Get Started!

Features

Corporate entity formation

We help clients with promising products form a limited liability company (LLC), with us as legal partners. We will make sure to give our clients the highest percentage of equity in the company, along with total control of the operations.

Product design & creation

We help our clients with the design and the creation of their product. If you already have an idea but don't know where to start, we're here for you. If you don't know how to build out your idea and deploy it, we got this!

Marketing

Utilizing our audience and growth on social media, we can help promote and advertise your product, and help drive traffic to your creation. We can also help you start your own independent social media channels and grow your audience!

Connections

We have a large pool of connections, ranging from nonprofit organizations, to experts in certain fields. We can give you a step up when it comes to grabbing partnerships and advisory. We can set your team up for success.

Miscellaneous services

Some skills are hard to come across. If you need a logo, need to get ideas for UI design, need to shoot a video, or create an original illustration, we've got your back. We have amazing talent that can help you immediately!

Talent provider

Looking for talent? Need someone skilled in a certain field to join your team? You can utilize our social media following to help recruit certain talents and add them to your team!

Complete security

We pledge to have both parties sign non-disclosure agreements, so you feel safe and comfortable discussing your ideas with us and anyone else within Launch Tech! We want to help you, and ensure confidentiality at the same time.
