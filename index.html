About Launch Tech

Launch Tech is a SaaS (Software as a Service) product holding company, dedicated to launching real-world software applications. We create products that are useful, meaningful, and applicable to different people and different situations around the world, and we partner with other organizations to make this into a reality!

Though we are a SaaS product company, we try to open with our processes, builds, and deadlines. We have a calendar that shares all of the things we're working on, and when certain events/product-builds will start, end, and be deployed.
